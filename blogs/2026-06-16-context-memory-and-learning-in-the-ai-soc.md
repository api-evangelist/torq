Torq argues the model was never what held the SOC back; the sharpest LLM still won't know your environment, your team's past calls, or where they draw the line on risk. This post introduces the layer beneath the agents through three foundational elements: the Torq Context Graph for environmental grounding, Torq Recall for memory, and Torq Reflex for decision-making judgment.
